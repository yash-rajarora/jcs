Wanna Be Our Campus Ambassador?


Students who are enthusiastic about cutting-edge technologies have a platform to promote the JECRC Cloud Summit'22 in their institution or university through the JCS'22 Campus Ambassador Program. You will serve as the JECRC Cloud Summit's spokesperson, face, and evangelist on your campus along with engaging alongside global tech titans. Through this incredibly competitive programme, you'll develop professionally and earn important job experience while making a significant contribution to the Summit's success.

Join Now




























Our mission is to empower students from different backgrounds and encourage them about the hottest subjects in the tech business today, gain practical advice on how to execute your ideas and plans, and have an unforgettable experience that will benefit you long beyond the event.
